County, State and US Comparison with Min & Max Values

This visualization charts the means for county, state, and US low birth weight percentages from 1998 to 2015. Included are the minimum and maximum values.


Observations

  • Immediately noticable is the fact that the averages over the 18 year period all (except the US data) cluster around the 6 percent line.
  • Similar to what we saw with the choropleth map, Washington county and Multnomah county seem to share a minimum but not an mean or maximum.
  • Portland Metropolitan area and surrounding counties and also including the two states as a whole are not bring up the US average.
